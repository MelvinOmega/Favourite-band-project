ELANI BAND

Elani are a Kenyan African pop group from Nairobi consisting of three members; Wambui Ngugi, Brian Chweya and Maureen Kunga. The trio released their first single "Jana Usiku" in November 2013 which became a great success. In 2014, they released numerous singles in the making of their album Barua ya Dunia.
They met in January 2008 at Alliance Francaise in Nairobi.
